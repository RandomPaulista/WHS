Treaties and Agreements

Tokyo Trade Treaty

June 1836 | Japan and British Empire

- The UK will respect Japan as an equal trade partner.
- Japan will lift the bullion exchange ban on the UK specifically.
- Japan will prioritize exports from the UK for steel, coal, gears, advanced tools, and other industrial machinery.

Potaro Treaty

June 1836 | New Granada and British Empire

- The United Kingdom will give up claims in the Essequibo region north of the Potaro River & give up Pirara
- New Granada will support the United Kingdoms claims in Miskito & Honduras

Treaty of Turku

June 1836 | Russian Empire and British Empire

- The United Kingdom will give up claims in Alaska to Russia
- Russia will support British claims in Oregon
- Russia & the United Kingdom will promise to ease tensions
- in Central Asia, the UK & Russia will agree to not invade past the Farsi areas of Afghanistan

Treaty of Austin

June 1837 | California Republic, Republic of Texas, United States and Mexico

- The Republic of Texas will become a state under the US with all its claims pushed
- California will become a state under the US with all its claims pushed
- The United States will annex New Mexico, Utah, Nevada, Arizona, the rest of Colorado, Oklahoma, Wyoming and Kansas. Baja California is to be annexed as part of the California Republic
- Mexico drops all claims on American territory

Treaty of Florence

September 1837 | Sardinia-Piedmont and Roman Republic

- The Kingdom of Sardinia-Piedmont and the Roman Republic will unify into the Kingdom of Italy.
- The Kingdom of Italy will become a Parliamentary Constitutional Monarchy, where both the republican and imperial sectors of government have power.

Treaty of Naples

September 1840 | Kingdom of Italy and Kingdom of Two Sicilies

- The Kingdom of Two Sicilies will be fully annexed and integrated into the Kingdom of Italy.
- The kingdom will be divided into two distinct parts, Naples and Sicily.
- Naples will retain their monarchy as yet another noble house within our kingdom.
- Sicily will become a distinct region with a status similar to that of the former Roman Republic.
- The capital of the Kingdom of Italy will be moved to Rome as it is now the most central location.

Anglo-Italian Treaty

September 1840 | Kingdom of Italy and British Empire

- The Ionian Islands will be handed to Italy
- the United Kingdom will support Italy in any conflict against neighbouring countries
- the United Kingdom will support Italy in any African ventures

Treaty of Alexandria

March 1841 | Kingdom of Italy and Khedivate of Egypt

- Italy will assist Egypt in obtaining full independence from the Ottoman Empire
- Egypt will allow Italy to station troops within their lands
- Egypt will recognize Italian control over Tunisia and Libya
- Italy will assist Egypt in army training, industrialization, and gaining worldwide recognition
- Italy will gain some trading rights within Egypt
- Egypt will guarantee the rights and freedoms of Christians and Jews within Egypt
- Egypt and Italy will allign against the Ottoman Empire

Treaty of Venice

June 1841 | Austrian Empire and Italy

- The Austrian Empire and the Kingdom of Italy shall commit to the mutual defense and territorial integrity of each other in times of conflict.
- If any further amendments must be made to the treaty, the Austrian and Italian heads of state shall convene in the city of Venice to negotiate.
